Frequently Asked Questions

here are ten frequently asked questions (FAQs) for the "Don't Do It Yourself (DDIY)" cleaning service company:

What services does DDIY offer?

DDIY offers a comprehensive range of cleaning services tailored for commercial spaces. This includes regular cleaning, deep cleaning, move-in/out cleaning, and specialized services like carpet cleaning and window washing.

How do I schedule a cleaning service with DDIY?

Scheduling a cleaning service with DDIY is simple! You can contact our customer service team directly via phone or email to arrange a convenient appointment.

Are your cleaning products environmentally friendly?

Yes, at DDIY, we prioritize the use of eco-friendly cleaning products that are safe for your family, pets, and the environment. We believe in delivering high-quality results while being environmentally conscious.

Are your cleaners background checked and insured?

Absolutely. Every DDIY cleaner undergoes a rigorous background check and is fully insured and bonded to ensure the safety and security of your office.

What if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning service?

Customer satisfaction is our top priority. If you're not entirely satisfied with our service, please contact us within 24 hours of the cleaning, and we'll be happy to address any concerns and ensure your complete satisfaction.

Do I need to provide cleaning equipment and supplies?

No need! DDIY brings our own high-quality cleaning equipment and supplies to every appointment. You just need to provide access to your premises.

What areas do you serve?

DDIY proudly serves anywhere in the Atlanta area

How do you ensure my privacy and security during the cleaning process?

We highly value your privacy and security. Our cleaners are trained to respect your space and confidentiality. We also have strict protocols in place to safeguard your personal belongings and information.

What are your pricing and payment options?

Our pricing is transparent and competitive. We offer flexible payment options, and our rates depend on the type of service required and the size of the space. You can find detailed pricing information on our website or by contacting our team.

Is DDIY available for one-time cleaning services or only on a recurring basis?

We cater to both one-time cleaning needs and recurring cleaning services. Whether you need a single deep clean or wish to schedule regular maintenance cleanings, DDIY is here to accommodate your requirements.
